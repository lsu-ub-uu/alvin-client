About

Alvin is a platform for publishing and long-term preservation of digitised cultural heritage material from library, archive and museum collections in Sweden and Norway.

The system was developed at Uppsala University Library and has been in operation since 2014. It is led by a consortium consisting of the university libraries in Uppsala, Lund and Gothenburg.

In addition to these, there are about 70 other institutions that have published material in Alvin.
